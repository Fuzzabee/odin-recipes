Brussels Sprouts

Description

Brussels sprouts are a very divisive vegetable. If you've ever tried them, you either loved them or hated them. When I first tried them, they were boiled, had no seasoning, and tasted horrid. Once I tried roasted brussels sprouts, though, it was a game changer. Even if you have tried them before, I highly recommend trying them again roasted, as they are a completely different food when cooked correctly.

If you're still not convinced, you can mix these guys with some bacon or drizzle on some honey to create something even more appetizing!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb brussels sprouts
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 5 diced slices of bacon (Optional)
  • 2-3 tbsp of honey (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Cut brussels sprouts in half length-wise and toss in a bowl with the oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Spread evenly onto a baking sheet and cook for 15 minutes
  4. After 15 minutes, mix up the sprouts on the baking sheet and check the color, we are looking for dark brown, almost black
  5. If not done cooking, check every 3 or 4 minutes until dark brown
  6. Toss with any additional toppings you might have and server immediately
